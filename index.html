About Art's Arc Magazine Photoshoot

Hi, my name is Bruno also known as "the art god" and I'm a digital art photographer. Over the years I've worked with several models (upcoming and professional) and so many want to be seen or heard On the strength of this, I present to you Art's Arc magazine, a groundbreaking afro street wears fashion photography publication dedicated to showcasing the "alternative" fashion trend and designed to provide aspiring models a voice. interested participants are entitled to two pictures and are expected to show up with their outfits. style of the shoot is Alternative fashion (Alt).
